Skills and Jobs

What position description would be appropriate?

A position description that describes the importance of working in a team, one that explains the roles that each different job will take, the duties the job has and the importance of it in delivering the final product. The position description must explain what the project is and how it works. It will also demonstrate the culture in the workplace and how the workday is going to be with what jobs there is to do. Write 4 position descriptions for people that you would employ to take your project to the next phase. Software Engineer/Designer - A Software Engineer and Designer role is all about being able to program a new app that will be used alongside our new Smart Drop Box device. The app is called ‘Smart Drop’ and has been designed as a workable prototype that is accessible using Figma. This fun and exciting opportunity requires at least a Bachelor of Information Technology and to have proficiency in C++ and has some sort of experience in UX design. A Software Engineer and Designer will work in a brilliant team with a whole range of different roles which will result in valuable skills, with a great culture in the workplace, everyone respects each other and with once-a-week activities to get people to know each other and forge a greater team! Documentation Writer – A Documentation Writer role is about preparing and writing the documentation for the app and the Smart Drop Box product. As a Documentation Writer your role would require you to be in communication with all other people and their roles to discuss and communicate how the project is going to work and you will have to plan and write the instructions on how the software works. This fun and exciting job opportunity requires a Diploma of Information Technology with at least 2 years in the Information Technology Industry (Note: That this job requires you to pass our ‘Language Test’ to make sure that the applicant can write long documentation free from any grammatical errors. With a great culture in the workplace, everyone respects each other and with once-a-week activities to get people to know each other and forge a great team! Hardware Engineer – A Hardware Engineer is an important role that emphasis on being able to create the actual product from a model that was designed in the past of how the actual product should look like. You are required to be in contact with other members of the group in order to coordinate what must be done and to make sure that at the end the ‘Smart Drop Box’ is created, and the hardware is all done with the Software Engineer making sure it is programmed to work correctly and is in sync with the app that goes along with it and with the Documentation writer, the documentation must be correctly written to explain everything about the Hardware and how it works. This opportunity requires, a Bachelor of Computer Engineering and any amount of prior experience is acceptable. With a great culture in the workplace, everyone respects each other and with once-a-week activities to get people to know each other and forge a great team! Lead Engineer – A lead Engineer role is to make sure that everything is working as plan you will be watching over both the Hardware Engineer and Software Engineer and help them out to get the project done. This job requires a bachelor's in engineering preferred to be in Software or Hardware engineering and at least 5 years' experience in programming (preferred to have knowledge of C++) and putting together electronics and hardware. With a great culture in the workplace, everyone respects each other and with once-a-week activities to get people to know each other and forge a great team!
